Flow arts such as gloving started as a drug related paraphernalia tied to the rave scene, but now there are competitions based on dance and performance scorecards that runs online and local communities. 'Gloving is not a crime' is a movement that's trying to make that disconnect between the utilization of flow arts as a performing arts from drug related paraphernalia. Participating on a gloving team, Infinite Movement, is well regarded as the number one gloving team in the world based on Gloving.com's point standard where local competitions and online competitions are taken into consideration. Having a reputation world wide, we try our best to build and mentor our local community and share our practice and learning habits with others around the world. More revently we ran a mentorship program where we took learning glovers and taught them our perspective of showmanship, presence and technique across the span of a year through weekly 'assignments' which them submitted as practice videos. Aside from that, our team is very close knit and keeping strong bonds between us allows us to push our creative boundaries.
